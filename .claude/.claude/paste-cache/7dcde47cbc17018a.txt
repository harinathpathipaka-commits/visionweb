Specific Technical Questions
On the scoring engine: You store context_embedding: Vec<f32> (768-dim) per decision. After 10,000 decisions you have 768 × 4 bytes × 10,000 = ~30MB. Fine. But your vector search QueryBestActions(context_embedding, k=5) — what distance metric? Cosine similarity? What's the query latency target at 10k, 100k, 1M records? LanceDB is good but this should be in the performance targets table.
On the Cross-Eye Coordinator: It identifies contradictions — "Vision says button visible, DOM says disabled." What happens when contradiction is found? The plan says "Produces unified SituationalSnapshot" but doesn't define the resolution strategy. Which eye wins? Is it always Vision over DOM? Or context-dependent?
On Goal Decomposer: Uses LLM to decompose a goal into sub-goals. But what's the sub-goal schema? You have SubGoal with success_criteria: list[str]. How are these verified? Is each success criterion checked by the Goal Verifier against the DiffReport? This connection is not explicit in the data flow.
